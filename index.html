01 / Who We Are

Built from Bedrock

Blackrock Dynamics was founded in May 2026 on a simple conviction: access to space should be reliable, repeatable, and affordable. We are a small, focused team of engineers moving fast toward our first flight.

Rather than spending a decade developing hardware from scratch, we acquired the flight-ready Phoebe launch vehicle from Saturn Aerospace. That puts us on track to reach orbit within months of our founding, not years.

  • Reliability first Every system is tested beyond its rated limits before flight.
  • Proven hardware We build on flight-tested platforms, starting with Phoebe.
  • Rapid iteration A small team with a short path from decision to action.

02 / Our Vehicle

Phoebe
